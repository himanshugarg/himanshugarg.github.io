Chapter 1EPUB Math Sample

This ebook illustrates EPUB Math, the proposed best practices format for representing mathematics notation in EPUB books. The proposed EPUB Math specification is available on the Design Science web site.

The chief idea of EPUB Math is to represent equations in three formats --  MathML, SVG, and an image --  enclosed in an <ops:switch> element.  The sample content below is encoded in this way. Though in practice all three encodings would represent the same notation, here each format is a different equation to make it easier to determine which format a given reading system currently displays.

  • The MathML equation is "x squared plus y minus z".
  • The SVG equation is "sigma subscript i superscript 2".
  • The PNG image is "alpha squared plus one".
