```
The Mask

For the Amazon interview,
I wore a mask.
My friend from Amazon,
Answered every question.

The interviewer then asked,
If I had any.
"I think we've met before.
Is your name Honey?"

"No, it is Alexa,
Amazon, Alexa!
Didn't your friend from Amazon,
Tell you about me?"
```
